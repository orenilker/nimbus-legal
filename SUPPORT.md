# Nimbus: Weather & Style – Support

Thanks for using **Nimbus: Weather & Style**!  
If you have any questions, feedback, or issues, you can reach us using the information below.

## Contact

- **Email**: support@nimbusapp.example  
- **Response time**: We aim to respond within 2–3 business days.

## Frequently Asked Topics

- **Bug reports**: Please include your device model, iOS version, and a short description of the problem.
- **Feature requests**: Tell us what you’d like to see in future versions of Nimbus.
- **Subscription & purchase issues**: Include your App Store country and the Apple ID you used for the purchase (do not send your password).
